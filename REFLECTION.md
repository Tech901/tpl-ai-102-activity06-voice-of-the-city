# Activity 6 — Voice of the City: Reflection

Answer these questions after completing the activity. There are no wrong answers — the goal is to deepen your understanding.

## Technical Reflection

1. **Speech Recognition Accuracy**: What factors affected the accuracy of your speech-to-text transcriptions? How might background noise, accents, or audio quality impact a real 311 call center?

2. **CLU vs. Keyword Matching**: Compare the CLU model's intent classification with a simple keyword-based approach. When would each be more appropriate?

3. **SSML Control**: How does SSML give you more control over synthesized speech compared to plain text TTS? Give a specific example from your implementation.

## Design Reflection

4. **Multilingual Access**: Memphis has a growing multilingual population. How does speech translation make city services more accessible? What limitations did you observe?

5. **Knowledge Base Design**: How did the structure of the FAQ pairs affect the quality of answers? What would you change about the knowledge base for production use?

## Exam Connection

6. **AI-102 Relevance**: Which exam objectives from Domain 5 (NLP Solutions) did this activity help you practice? How confident do you feel about Speech Services and CLU questions on the exam?
